Description
Global Alignment of Immunization safety Assessment in pregnancy (GAIA) consists of standards, tools and guidance to harmonize data on immunization in pregnancy for low income countries.

GAIA is updated annually as part of the NCI Thesaurus in the Metathesaurus.
