Authority
The U.S. National Cancer Institute (NCI) produces the Clinical Trials Reporting Program (CTRP).

Description
CTRP is a database that includes information for all NCI-supported interventional clinical trials, that were open to patient accrual as of January 2009.

Metathesaurus Update Frequency
CTRP is included annually as part of the NCI Thesaurus in the Metathesaurus.
