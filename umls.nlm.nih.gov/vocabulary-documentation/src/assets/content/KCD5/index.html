Authority
The Korean National Health Information Task Force produces the Korean Standard Classification of Disease Version 5 (KCD5).

Description
This is the Korean translation of the International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems, 10th Revision (ICD-10). ICD-10 is produced by the World Health Organization (WHO). For more information about ICD-10, see the ICD-10 Web site.
