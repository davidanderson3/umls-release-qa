Term Type

Term types are extracted from field 13 of RXNCONSO.RRF and RXNCONSOOCD.RRF.

Suppressibility

For selected term types (e.g., "BPCK", "DF", "GPCK", "SBDC", "SBDF", "SBD", "SCDC", "SCD", "SY"), the suppressibility value can be either "O" or "N". All others, the value is "N". Suppressibility values are extracted from field 17 of RXNCONSO.RRF.
