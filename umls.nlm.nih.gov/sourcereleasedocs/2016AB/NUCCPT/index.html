Authority
The National Uniform Claim Committee (NUCC) produces the Health Care Provider Taxonomy. The American Medical Association (AMA) chairs the committee with help from the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services (CMS).

Description
The National Uniform Claim Committee - Health Care Provider Taxonomy (NUCCPT) is a standardized data set that is used for the transmission of claims and encounter information to third party payers.
