Source Provided Files:


The Metathesaurus uses files submitted in TREF (Terminology Representation and Exchange Format).  These files are produced by the National Center for Health Statistics (NCHS) for Metathesaurus processing, and are not currently available publically.  TREF was developed by the NLM and is a subset of the Rich Release Format (RRF) – the format in which the UMLS Metathesaurus is released.  The following files are submitted for ICD10CM:
   
MRCONSO.TREF
MRDOC.TREF
MRREL.TREF
MRSAB.TREF
MRSAT.TREF

In addition, the following mapping files were downloaded from http://www.cms.gov/ICD10/11b1_2011_ICD10CM_and_GEMs.asp#TopOfPage:
10cm to 9cm gem.txt
9cm to 10cm gem.txt
Diagnosis Reimbursement mapping .txt

Mappings (MRMAP):

  (return to top)

Three Mapsets ("XM" atoms, along with associated attributes and mappings), are included for ICD-10-CM. These are extracted from the General Equivalence Mappings (GEMs) and reimbursement mappings.

Note:  The original mapping files do not include decimal points for the ICD-9-CM and ICD-10-CM codes.  For clarity, decimals are added appropriately to the MRMAP.RRF representation, e.g. "A021" is converted to "A02.1" for use as a FROMID or TOID.
