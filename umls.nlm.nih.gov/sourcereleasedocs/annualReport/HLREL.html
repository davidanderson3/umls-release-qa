HLREL_1998 - ICPC2E-ICD10 relationships from Dr. Henk Lamberts, 1998

HLREL_1998 - ICPC2E-ICD10 relationships from Dr. Henk Lamberts, 1998

Authority
The International Classification of Primary Care, Second Edition, Electronic (ICPC2E) - International Statistical Classification of Diseases and Related Health Problems (ICD10) Relationships were developed by Dr. Henk Lamberts at the University of Amsterdam.

Purpose
ICPC2E-ICD10 Relationships is used to code data about patients’ diagnoses.

Description
ICPC2E-ICD10 Relationships is a set of relationships between ICPC2E and ICD10 concepts. No source atoms are included.

ICPC2E-ICD10 Relationships also includes relationships between ICD9CM and ICD10.

Audience
ICPC2E-ICD10 Relationships is used by general/family practice and primary care physicians.

Metathesaurus Update Frequency
ICPC2E-ICD10 Relationships was last updated in the Metathesaurus in 2001.
