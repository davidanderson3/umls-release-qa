Authority
The Norwegian Knowledge Centre for the Health Services (Nasjonalt kunnskapssenter for helsetjenesten) produces the Norwegian translation of Medical Subject Headings (MeSH®).

Description
This is the Norwegian translation of MeSH.

For more information about MeSH in the Metathesaurus, see the MeSH source synopsis.

Metathesaurus Update Frequency
MSHNOR is a new source in the 2012AA Metathesaurus release. MSHNOR will be updated annually in the Metathesaurus.
