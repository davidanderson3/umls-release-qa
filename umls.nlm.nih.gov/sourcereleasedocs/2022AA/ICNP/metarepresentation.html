VSAB: ICNP_2021

Summary of Changes

Under a September, 2021 agreement, ICNP will be fully integrated with SNOMEDSNOMED CT, ensuring that SNOMED CT continues to reflect the valuable contribution of nursing to health care. SNOMED International will be responsible for the distribution of ICNP, while ICN will retain ownership and control of its content.

The ICNP 2021 standalone release consists of a subset of SNOMED CT codes and strings. This data is represented in the UMLS Metathesaurus with SAB = "ICNP."
  • Deprecated TTYs: MTH_PT, XM

  • MTH_MAPFROMCOMPLEXITY,MTH_MAPFROMEXHAUSTIVE,MTH_MAPSETCOMPLEXITY, MTH_MAPTOCOMPLEXITY, MTH_MAPTOEXHAUSTIVE, TORSAB, TOVSAB
