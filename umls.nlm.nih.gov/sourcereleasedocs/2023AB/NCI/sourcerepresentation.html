VSAB: NCI2023_03D

General Notes/Comments:

NCI Thesaurus (NCIt) is a widely recognized standard for biomedical coding and reference. It covers vocabulary for clinical care, translational and basic research, public information and administrative activities and is used by a broad variety of public and private partners both nationally and internationally.
