Notes

In addition to the LNC SAB, LOINC includes nine subsources, which are assigned by an algorithm using field 43 (EXTERNAL_COPYRIGHT_NOTICE), field 9 (CLASS) and field 7 (METHOD_TYP).  The representation described below applies to all subsources.  Due to licensing restrictions, concepts from subsources TIMP (Test of Infant Motor Performance) and PROMIS (Patient Reported Outcomes Measurement System survey) are not included in Metathesaurus source processing.
