Description
The NCI Thesaurus incorporates a small set of cancer-related terms from the U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC).

Metathesaurus Update Frequency
The CDC terms are updated annually as part of the NCI Thesaurus in the Metathesaurus.
