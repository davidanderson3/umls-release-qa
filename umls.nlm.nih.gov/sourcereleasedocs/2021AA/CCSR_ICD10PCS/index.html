Authority
The Clinical Classifications Software Refined for ICD-10-PCS (CCSR_ICD10PCS) is produced by the Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ) as part of the Healthcare Cost and Utilization Project (HCUP).

Description
CCSR_ICD10PCS organizes ICD-10-PCS procedure codes into smaller, clinically meaningful, categories.

Update Frequency
CCSR_ICD10PCS is updated annually.

Metathesaurus Update Frequency
CCSR_ICD10PCS is updated annually in the Metathesaurus.
