Source-Provided Files: Summary

The files "ICNP-SCT DC.csv" and "ICNP-SCT IC.csv" consist of a subset of SNOMED CT codes and ICNP preferred terms

Source-Provided Files: Details

The following is a list of fields available for ICNP_2022 data in "ICNP-SCT DC.csv" and "ICNP-SCT IC.csv"

Field Representation
ICNP ID MRSAT.ATN = ICNP_ID
ICNP preferred term (Diagnosis/Outcome or Intervention) MRCONSO.STR
SCTID (SNOMED CT identifier) MRCONSO.CODE, MRCONSO.SCUI
SNOMED CT preferred term with semantic tag (SNOMED CT fully-specified name) Not directly processed
