VSAB:

Summary of Changes

Slight changes have been made to the column order and location of fields in the .xlsx file.  While this required minor changes to Metathesaurus source processing, the essential model is the same.

Minor changes have been made to the representation of ICNP to SNOMEDCT mappings:  based on information asserted by the source, the majority of mappings are represented with REL = "SY" rather than "RQ".

Mappings (MRMAP, MRSMAP):

  (return to top)

SNOMED CT mappings are extracted from the ICNP-SCT Mapping tab on the .xlsx file.   Mappings are also represented in MRREL.RRF, with RELA = "mapped_to"

The following REL values are assigned based on the value in the "Notes" field:
    SY:  <null> (empty cell)
    RB/RN:  Inexact match (broader) or Inexact match (narrower)
    XR (MRMAP.RRF, MRSMAP.RRF):  No match
