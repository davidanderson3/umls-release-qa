Authority
NCI_HL7 is produced by Health Level Seven International (HL7).

Purpose
NCI_HL7 supports the HL7 development process.

Description
NCI_HL7 is a representation of the HL7 clinical data (domains) and its related vocabulary.

Audience
NCI_HL7 is employed by HL7 Standards Developers, HL7 Standards Implementers, and users of healthcare system data and its communication.

Metathesaurus Update Frequency
NCI_HL7 is updated annually as part of the NCI Thesaurus in the Metathesaurus.
