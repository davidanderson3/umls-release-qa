Authority
NCI_NICHD is produced by National Institute of Child Health and Human Development (NICHD), National Children's Study (NCS), and NCI.

Purpose
NCI_NICHD provides standardized terminology for coding pediatric clinical trials and other research activities.

Description
NCI_NICHD is a core library of harmonized pediatric terms, beginning with a focus on neonatal and infant examination concepts as part of a broader terminology framework. Each term is represented as a separate concept, and assigned a unique code, term definition, and synonym/abbreviation, where required. Each newborn examination concept has an association relationship, Concept in Subset: Newborn Screening, for easy retrieval of the terminology.

Audience
NCI_NICHD is employed by the pediatric and neonatal research community.

Update Frequency
NCI_NICHD is updated monthly.

Metathesaurus Update Frequency
NCI_NICHD is updated annually as part of the NCI Thesaurus in the Metathesaurus.
