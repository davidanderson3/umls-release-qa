Authority
NCI_ZFin is produced by Zebrafish International Resource Center, Zebrafish Information Network (ZFIN).

Purpose
NCI_ZFin: (a) to develop and support integrated zebrafish genetic, genomic and developmental information, b) to facilitate the use of zebrafish as a model for human biology and c) to serve the needs of the research community.

Description
NCI_ZFin (the NCI-hosted Zebrafish Model Organism Database (Zebrafish)) is an ontology of zebrafish anatomical terms, including many definitions and synonyms.

Audience
NCI_ZFin is used by researchers for annotating gene expression and phenotypes, providing a link between these two types of data commonly used to study gene function.

Metathesaurus Update Frequency
NCI_ZFin is updated annually as part of the NCI Thesaurus in the Metathesaurus.
