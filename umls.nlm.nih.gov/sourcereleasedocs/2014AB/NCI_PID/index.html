Authority
NCI_PID (NCI/Nature Pathway Interaction Database) is produced and maintained by the Nature Publishing Group (NPG) and NCI.

Purpose
NCI_PID serves as a reference source of biomolecular interactions and for use to run computational analyses.

Description
NCI_PID is an NCI Thesaurus property that consists of identification codes for a pathways; links to information about known biomolecular interactions and key cellular processes assembled into signaling pathways. It can be used by the NCIt Browser to link a pathway concept to the source pathway information.

Audience
NCI_PID is employed by the cancer research community and others interested in cellular pathways, such as neuroscientists, developmental biologists, and immunologists.

Metathesaurus Update Frequency
NCI_PID is updated annually as part of the NCI Thesaurus in the Metathesaurus.
