Authority
Current Procedural Terminology (CPT®) is a product of the American Medical Association (AMA).

Purpose
CPT codes are used by health care professionals for billing of medical services and procedures to public and private health insurance programs.

Description
CPT is a list of descriptive terms and identifying numeric codes for medical services and procedures that are provided by physicians and health care professionals.

Audience
CPT is used by physicians, health practitioners, and health insurance organizations.

Update Frequency
CPT is updated annually by the AMA.

Metathesaurus Update Frequency
CPT is updated annually in the Metathesaurus.
