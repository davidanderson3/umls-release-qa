Description
BioCarta online maps of molecular pathways provides graphical models of molecular relationships, including proteomic and genomic information, as well as classical pathways and suggestions for new pathways.

Metathesaurus Update Frequency
BioCarta online maps of molecular pathways is updated annually as part of the NCI Thesaurus in the Metathesaurus.
