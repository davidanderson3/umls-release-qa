Authority
The National Center for Immunization and Respiratory Diseases (NCIRD) produces the HL7 Table 0227, Manufacturers of Vaccines (MVX).  The NCIRD is part of the U.S. Centers for Disease Control and Prevention (CDC).

Description
MVX includes manufacturers of active and inactive vaccines in the US.

An MVX code can be paired with a CVX (vaccine administered) code.
