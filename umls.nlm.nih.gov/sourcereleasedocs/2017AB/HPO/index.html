Purpose
HPO allows for large-scale computational analysis of the human phenome.

Description
HPO has over 11,000 terms that describe clinical abnormalities. It includes relationships, all of which are transitive and is-a relationships. Each term is assigned to one of the five subontologies:

  • Phenotypic abnormality,
  • Mode of Inheritance,
  • Clinical Modifier
  • Mortality/Aging
  • Frequency
