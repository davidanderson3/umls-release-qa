Authority
The American Psychiatric Assocation produces the Diagnostic and Statistical Manual of Mental Disorders, Fifth Edition (DSM-5).

Purpose
DSM-5 is used to diagnose as well as classify mental disorders. It allows for an objective assessment of symptoms in a range of clinical settings.

Description
DSM-5 has three parts: diagnostic classification, diagnostic criteria sets and descriptive text.
