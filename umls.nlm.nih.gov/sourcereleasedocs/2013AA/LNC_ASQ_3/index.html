Publisher
The Ages & Stages Questionnaire®, Third Edition, (ASQ-3) is published by the Paul H. Brookes Publishing Co., Inc.

Purpose
ASQ-3 is a survey for parents and caregivers that screens for developmental delays in young children. Parents or caregivers complete the survey; professionals provide score results. The target ages for the screening is between 4 months and 60 months.

Metathesaurus Scope
The Metathesaurus representation of the ASQ-3 is extracted from the LOINC release files.

Audience
The audience includes parents, early childhood educators, social workers, and clinical professionals.

Metathesaurus Update Frequency
This is a new source for the 2013AA Release. ASQ-3 will be updated in each Metathesaurus release as part of LOINC.
