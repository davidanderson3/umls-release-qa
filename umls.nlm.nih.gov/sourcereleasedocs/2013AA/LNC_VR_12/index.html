Description
The Veterans RAND 12 Item Health Survey (VR-12) is a self-administered survey that measures health related quality of life, estimates disease burden, and evaluates disease-specific benchmarks with other populations. The survey times correspond to principal physical and mental health domains.

Metathesaurus Scope
The Metathesaurus representation of VR-12 is extracted from the LOINC release files.

Metathesaurus Update Frequency
This is a new source for the 2013AA Release. VR-12 will be updated in each Metathesaurus release as part of LOINC.
