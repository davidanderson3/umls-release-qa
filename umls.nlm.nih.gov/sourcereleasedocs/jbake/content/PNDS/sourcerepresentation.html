Summary of Changes:

There have changes made to the layout of the Perioperative Nursing Data Set (PNDS) .xslx file that do not affect content. In addition, there is an new tab to represent "Nursing Problems." These codes are prefixed with "NP" and are represented with TTY = PT.

PNDS_4_2018 includes mappings to SNOMEDCT_US. These are represented in MRREL.RRF as well as in MRMAP.RRF and MRSMAP.RRF.

New TTY:
  • XM
New REL/RELA:
  • RQ|mapped_to/mapped_from
New ATNs:
  • FROMRSAB
  • FROMVSAB
  • MAPSETRSAB
  • MAPSETTYPE
  • MAPSETVERSION
  • MAPSETVSAB
  • MTH_MAPFROMCOMPLEXITY
  • MTH_MAPFROMEXHAUSTIVE

  • MTH_MAPTOEXHAUSTIVE
  • TORSAB
  • TOVSAB
  • XMAP
  • XMAPFROM
  • XMAPTO
Dropped ATNs:

  • RETIRED_CODE
