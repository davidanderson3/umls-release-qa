Authority
INC is a global collaboration formed to forge a predictable regulatory path for evaluating the safety and effectiveness of therapies for neonates.

Description
A terminology developed by the International Neonatal Consortium (INC). Operating in the pre-competitive space, INC addresses the need for measurement and assessment of clinical outcomes in neonates through teams that share data and expertise to advance regulatory science.

Metathesaurus Update Frequency
INC terms are updated annually as part of the NCI Thesaurus in the Metathesaurus.
