Authority
Clinical Classifications Software (CCS) for ICD-10-CM/PCS is produced by the Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ) as part of the Healthcare Cost and Utilization Project (HCUP).

Description
CCS organizes ICD-10/CM/PCS codes into large diagnosis groups to aid mortality data report and statistical analysis.

CCS is based on valid ICD-10/CM/PCS codes from September 2003.
