Authority
The Canonical Clinical Problem Statement System (CCPSS) was developed by Vanderbilt University in conjunction with the Emory University Department of Medicine and Medical Systems Development Corporation.

Purpose
CCPSS is used to describe and code patient problems in a clinically meaningful way.

Description
CCPSS is a list of problems, modifiers, and term categories. It contains over 15,000 problem terms and associated term frequencies derived from patient records.

Audience
CCPSS is used clinicians and informatics researchers.

Metathesaurus Update Frequency
CCPSS was last updated in the Metathesaurus in 2000.
