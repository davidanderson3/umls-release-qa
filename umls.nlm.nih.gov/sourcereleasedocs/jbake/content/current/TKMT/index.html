Synopsis Source Representation Metathesaurus Representation

Authority
The Korea Institute of Oriental Medicine produces the Traditional Korean Medical Terms.

Description
Traditional Korean Medical Terms is a Korean language source. It is built on 14 parent nodes and contains 370 concepts.

Metathesaurus Update Frequency
Traditional Korean Medical Terms are updated annually in the Metathesaurus, as resources permit.
