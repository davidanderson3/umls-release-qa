Source Provided Files:

The UMLS uses MedlinePlus xml files.

For 2022AB, the files used were:
  • mplus_topics_2022-11-01.xml
  • mplus_topic_groups_2022-11-01.xml

Atoms (MRCONSO):

Notes:

  • Only English language records (language="English") are processed for the Metathesaurus.  Spanish language records (language="Spanish") are not included.
  •  Even when duplicated, both ETs and SYs are included for complete representation of the source.

Hierarchy (MRHIER):

The hierarchy is created by connecting a health topic with its health topic group using <group id> tag values in mplus_topics_YYYY-MM-DD.xml.

Mappings (MRMAP):

No mappings are represented for MedlinePlus.
