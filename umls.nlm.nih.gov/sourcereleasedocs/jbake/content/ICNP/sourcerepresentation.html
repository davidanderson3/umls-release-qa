Summary of Changes:

Under a September, 2021 agreement, ICNP will be fully integrated with SNOMED CT, ensuring that SNOMED CT continues to reflect the valuable contribution of nursing to health care. SNOMED International will be responsible for the distribution of ICNP, while ICN will retain ownership and control of its content.

The ICNP 2021 standalone release consists of a subset of SNOMED CT codes and strings. This data is represented in the UMLS Metathesaurus with SAB = "ICNP."
  • Deprecated TTYs: MTH_PT, XM
  • Deprecated ATNs: FROMRSAB, FROMVSAB,ICNP_TYPE,MAPSETRSAB,MAPSETVERSION, MAPSETVSAB,
  • MTH_MAPFROMCOMPLEXITY,MTH_MAPFROMEXHAUSTIVE,MTH_MAPSETCOMPLEXITY, MTH_MAPTOCOMPLEXITY, MTH_MAPTOEXHAUSTIVE, TORSAB, TOVSAB
  • Deprecated REL value: SY
  • Deprecated RELA values: mapped_from/mapped_to; mth_british_form_of/mth_has_british_form
  • Deprecated REL values: PAR/CHD; there is no hierarchy for ICNP_2021
