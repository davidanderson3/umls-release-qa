Authority
The International Classification of Diseases, Tenth Revision, Australian Modification (ICD-10-AM) was developed and is maintained by the Australian National Centre for Classification in Health (NCCH).

Description
This is the Australian modification of ICD-10.

ICD-10-AM includes an Australian classification of procedures. This is a multi-axial classification based on the Australian Medicare Benefits Schedule (MBS) and is referred to as MBS-Extended (MBS-E).

For more information about ICD-10 in the Metathesaurus, see the ICD-10 source synopsis.

Update Frequency
ICD-10-AM is updated every two years.
