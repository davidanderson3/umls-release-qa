Authority
Ultrasound Structured Attribute Reporting (UltraSTAR) was developed at the Brigham and Women’s Hospital.

Purpose
UltraSTAR is a standardized vocabulary for recording sonographic outline reports.

Description
UltraSTAR contains less than 100 concepts and is focused on pelvic ultrasound information. It is used to generate preliminary outline reports that are then combined with other patient data into narrative reports.

Audience
UltraSTAR is used by sonographers and radiologists.

Metathesaurus Update Frequency
UltraSTAR was last updated in the Metathesaurus in 1995.
