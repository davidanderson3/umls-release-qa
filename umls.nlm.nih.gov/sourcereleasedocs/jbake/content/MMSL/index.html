Purpose
The Lexicon is used to improve patient safety when drugs are prescribed.

Description
The Lexicon is a foundational database with comprehensive drug product and disease nomenclature information. It includes drug names, drug product information, disease names, coding systems such as ICD-9-CM and NDC, generic names, brand names and common abbreviations. A comprehensive list of standard or customized disease names and ICD-9 codes is also included.

Metathesaurus Scope
The Metathesaurus representation of the Lexicon includes only branded clinical drugs, clinical drugs, generic drugs, ingredients, medical supplies, and brand names.

The Metathesaurus representation of the Lexicon contains approximately:

  • 29,571 branded clinical drugs
  • 22,270 brand names
  • 13,362 clinical drugs
  • 3,590 generic drugs
  • 3,082 ingredients
  • 12,193 medical supplies

Audience
The Lexicon is used by physicians, hospitals, pharmacies, health websites, and consumers.

Update Frequency
The Lexicon is updated monthly.

Metathesaurus Update Frequency
The Lexicon is updated in each Metathesaurus release as part of RxNorm.
