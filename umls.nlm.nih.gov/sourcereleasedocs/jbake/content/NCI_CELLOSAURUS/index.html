Description
NCI_CELLOSAURUS (Cellosaurus) is a knowledge resource on cell lines and aims to describe all cell lines used in biomedical research. Its scope encompasses both vertebrates and invertebrates. Cellosaurus is a key resource to help researchers identify potentially contaminated/misidentified cell lines, thus contributing to improving the quality of research in the life sciences.

Metathesaurus Update Frequency
Cellosaurus is updated annually as part of the NCI Thesaurus in the Metathesaurus.
