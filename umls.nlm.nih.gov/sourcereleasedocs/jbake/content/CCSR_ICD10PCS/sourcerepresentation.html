Summary of Changes

  • CCSR_ICD10PCS is a new SAB, but it is an update to CCS_10. Previously CCS_10 mapped ICD10PCS codes to single-level and multi-level categories. Now CCSR_ICD10PCS maps ICD10PCS codes to CCSR_ICD10PCS categories, each of which is within one of 31 clinical domains.
  • Previously the mapping relationships used AUIs but they have been changed to use CODE.
  • Deprecated TTY:
    • MV: No multi-level system with additional procedure specificity has been developed for CCSR_ICD10PCS
  • Deprecated REL/RELA:
    • SY/has_single_level_category: Previously connected single-level CCS CATEGORY to MULTI CCS LVL 2 categories, which no longer exist
