Authority
The Clinical Classifications Software (CCS) for ICD-10-PCS is produced by the Agency for Healthcare Research and Quality (AHRQ) as part of the Healthcare Cost and Utilization Project (HCUP).

Description
CCS organizes ICD-10-PCS procedure codes into smaller, clinically meaningful, categories.

Update Frequency
CCS for ICD-10-PCS is updated annually.

Metathesaurus Update Frequency
CCS for ICD-10-PCS is updated annually in the Metathesaurus.
