Description
NCI_ICDC (NCI Integrated Canine Data Commons) data supports research into the feasibility of pet dogs with spontaneous cancers as models of human disease to evaluate new drugs, immunotherapeutic agents and treatments for further development for human cancer patients.

Metathesaurus Update Frequency
NCI Integrated Canine Data Commons is updated annually as part of the NCI Thesaurus in the Metathesaurus.
