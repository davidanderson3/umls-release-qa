Source Provided Files:


The Metathesaurus uses files submitted in TREF (Terminology Representation and Exchange Format).  These files are produced by the National Center for Health Statistics (NCHS) for Metathesaurus processing, and are not currently available publically.  TREF was developed by the NLM and is a subset of the Rich Release Format (RRF) – the format in which the UMLS Metathesaurus is released.  The following files are submitted for ICD10CM:
   
MRCONSO.TREF
MRDOC.TREF
MRREL.TREF
MRSAB.TREF
MRSAT.TREF

In addition, the following mapping files were downloaded from http://www.cms.gov/ICD10/12_2010_ICD_10_CM.asp:
2010_I10gem.txt
2010_I9gem.txt
4_reimb_map_dx_2010.txt

Identifiers:


Identifiers are assigned as follows:
  • CODE: MRCONSO.TREF.DUI
  • SAUI: Not Applicable
  • SCUI: Not Applicable
  • SDUI: MRCONSO.TREF.DUI
