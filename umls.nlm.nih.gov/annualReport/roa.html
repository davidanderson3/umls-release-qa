SNOMED CT Route of Administration Subset

The SNOMED CTÂ® Route of Administration subset is a listing of the current set of terms related to the location of administration for clinical therapeutics. The purpose of the Route of Administration is to define a SNOMED CT subset, a portion of which can be used in the Drug Listing section of Structured Product Labeling (SPL), or for documentation and encoding of clinical information regarding substance administrations.
